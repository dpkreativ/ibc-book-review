Kill Me Quick

by Meja Mwangi

This fast-paced novel follows two young men struggling to survive in Nairobi after failing to find employment. As desperation grows, their moral boundaries are tested. With gritty realism and sharp dialogue, Meja Mwangi captures the harsh realities of urban poverty, disillusionment, and lost dreams.
