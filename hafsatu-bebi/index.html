Hafsatu Bebi

by Fatima Bala

Hafsatu Bebi by Fatima Bala is a novel about Hafsatu Gaya, a 22-year-old fashion entrepreneur in Abuja whose seemingly perfect life unravels after a reckless, fateful decision. The story, told across two timelines (late 1990s and present), explores themes of identity, deeply buried secrets, and the contrast between public personas and private truths. It highlights her journey of navigating complex family dynamics, maternal pressure, and a long-term relationship, ultimately revealing a, poignant, multigenerational narrative about love and resilience.
